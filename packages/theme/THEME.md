# Goldshore Theme Guidelines

## Color Palette

### Brand Colors
- **Matte Grey**: `#2A2A2A` (Surface, Backgrounds)
- **Silver**: `#C0C0C0` (Text Secondary, Borders)
- **Alcantara**: `#1C1C1C` (Main Background, Deep Contrast)
- **Utility Orange**: `#FF6600` (Accents, Buttons, Highlights)
- **Military Green**: `#4B5320` (Status, Subtle backgrounds)
- **Sports Car Green**: `#004225` (Success, "Go" signals)
- **Neon Yellow**: `#CCFF00` (High contrast highlights, Warnings)

## Typography
- **Base**: 'Inter', system-ui
- **Mono**: 'JetBrains Mono', monospace

## Design Language
- **Aesthetic**: Military x Tech, Sports Car
- **Key Elements**: Sharp edges, high contrast, functional minimalism.
